About Me

Thank you for joining the creation nation. My name is N.M. Porterfield and if you’re here, that means I’m on my way to becoming your favorite author (if I’m not already). I write in a variety of different genres(Action, Sci-if, Fantasy, Romance, Horror) I also write in a variety of different mediums(Comic books, poetry, music, and screenplays). My belief is that creativity is not an abstract quality that only a few have, but an internal part of everyone’s existence.
